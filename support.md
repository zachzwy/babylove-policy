# Help & Support

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I log an activity?
On the Home screen, long-press any skill node (e.g. NOURISHMENT, REST, GROWTH) to open the activity menu. Select the activity type, fill in the details, and tap Save. Each log earns XP for your baby.

### How do I use the timer for sleep, nursing, or bath?
Long-press the relevant skill node and select a timed activity (e.g. Nursing, Sleep, Bath). The floating timer card appears on screen and keeps running while you navigate the app. Tap Stop when done to save the session automatically.

### How do milestones get unlocked?
Tap a skill node to open its summary. Milestones are listed there — tap any milestone to review it and unlock it manually once your baby has achieved it. Unlocking a milestone typically awards 10–20 XP and adds it to the Hall of Fame.

### Can I create my own milestones?
Yes! Tap the skill icon at the top of any category in the Skill Map to add your own custom milestones. User-created milestones award 5 XP each (limited to the first 5 per category). Like standard milestones, they always award XP and aren't subject to the daily 30 XP activity limit.

### What are XP and levels?
Every logged activity and unlocked milestone earns XP. As XP accumulates your baby levels up, earning a new title at each level. Your weekly XP and activity breakdown are shown on the Stats screen.

### What are daily quests?
Each day a quest card appears on the Home screen with a bonus XP challenge (e.g. "Log 3 diaper changes today"). Complete the quest before midnight to earn the bonus. New quests are generated daily.

### Why do I get different XP amounts for logs?
Base XP is earned for logging activities (e.g., Feeding, Sleep, Diaper). Additional XP is earned via bonuses:
- **Photo Bonus (+1 XP):** Attach a photo to the log.
- **Early Bird Bonus (+1 XP):** Activity logged before 6 AM.
- **Combo Bonus (+2 XP):** Third log of the same type in a day.
- **Quest Bonus:** Log counts toward an active daily quest.

**You may earn 0 XP if:**
- Logs of the same type are less than 15 minutes apart.
- You've logged the same type 10 times in a single day.
- You've already earned 30 XP total for the day (excluding milestone XP).

### How do I switch between canvas and list view?
Use the toggle button at the bottom of the Home screen to switch between the interactive skill tree canvas and the expandable list view. In the list view you can also drag and reorder milestones within each skill section.

### How do I track growth percentiles?
Long-press the GROWTH node and select Weight or Height to log a measurement. The GROWTH node summary shows charts with WHO/CDC percentile bands so you can track trends over time.
