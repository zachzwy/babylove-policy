# Privacy Policy

**BabySkillMap**  
**Last updated:** April 14, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction
BabySkillMap ("we," "our," or "us") is a baby tracking application designed to help parents and caregivers log and monitor their baby's daily activities, growth, and milestones. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By using BabySkillMap, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
*   **Account information:** Email address, display name, and profile photo when you create an account.
*   **Baby profile:** Baby's name, date of birth, sex, birth weight, birth height, and photo.
*   **Activity logs:** Feeding records (nursing duration, bottle volume, pump yield, solid foods), sleep sessions, diaper changes, growth measurements (weight, height, head circumference), health records (temperature, medications, doctor visits), and general activity logs.
*   **Milestones:** Developmental milestones your baby has achieved, including any photos or videos you attach.
*   **Notes:** Free-text notes you add to any log entry.

### Information Collected Automatically
*   **Authentication tokens:** Managed by Firebase Authentication to keep you signed in securely.
*   **App usage data:** Firebase may collect anonymized usage and performance data to help maintain service reliability. See [Firebase Privacy & Security](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) for details.

### Information from Third-Party Sign-In
If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from those providers. We do not receive your password. Those sign-ins are governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use your information solely to provide and improve the BabySkillMap app:
*   Display your baby's profile, activity history, growth charts, and milestone tree.
*   Calculate XP, levels, streaks, and daily quests within the app.
*   Enable caregiver sharing so invited family members can view and log data for the same baby profile.
*   Send local push notifications (e.g., timer reminders) that you can enable or disable at any time in your device settings.
*   Respond to feedback or support requests you send to us.

**We do not use your information for advertising, sell it to third parties, or share it with any third party except as described in Section 4.**

---

## 4. How We Share Your Information

### Caregiver Sharing
If you generate an invite code and share it with another person, that person gains access to your baby's profile data (including activity logs, milestones, and photos) for as long as the caregiver link is active. You can revoke access at any time from the Profile screen.

### Service Providers
We use the following third-party services to operate the app:
*   **Provider:** Google Firebase
    *   **Purpose:** Authentication, database (Firestore), and file storage.
    *   **Privacy Policy:** [https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
*   **Provider:** Google (Sign in with Google)
    *   **Purpose:** Optional sign-in method.
    *   **Privacy Policy:** [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request (e.g., a court order or government demand).

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security
*   All data is stored in **Google Firebase** (Firestore and Firebase Storage), hosted in the United States.
*   Data is encrypted in transit using TLS and encrypted at rest by Firebase.
*   Access to your data is controlled by Firebase Security Rules: only your account (and caregivers you have explicitly invited) can read or write your baby's data.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy
BabySkillMap is intended for use by parents and caregivers (adults aged 13 or older). The app collects information about babies at the direction of a parent or guardian, but it is not directed at children themselves and does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information without parental consent, please contact us promptly.

---

## 7. Photos and Media
Photos and videos you attach are uploaded to Firebase Storage and associated with your account. They are accessible only to you and your linked caregivers. Once you delete a photo from within the app, it is permanently removed from Firebase Storage.

---

## 8. Push Notifications
BabySkillMap may send local push notifications (e.g., timers). These are scheduled on your device and do not involve sending your data to a remote server. You can disable these in your device's Settings app.

---

## 9. Your Rights and Choices
Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:
*   **Access:** View all data stored in your account within the app.
*   **Correction:** Edit your account or baby profile from the Profile screen.
*   **Deletion:** Delete your account via **Profile → Account Settings → Delete Account**. This is permanent.
*   **Data portability:** Contact us to request an export of your data.
*   **Withdraw consent:** Stop using the app and delete your account at any time.

---

## 10. Data Retention
Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is permanently removed from Firebase within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for service analytics.
