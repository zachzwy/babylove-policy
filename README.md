Privacy Policy

  BabySkillMap
  Last updated: April 12, 2026

  ---
  1. Introduction

  BabySkillMap ("we," "our," or "us") is a baby tracking application designed to help parents and caregivers log and monitor their
  baby's daily activities, growth, and milestones. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your
  information when you use our mobile application.

  By using BabySkillMap, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

  ---
  2. Information We Collect

  Information You Provide

  - Account information: Email address, display name, and profile photo when you create an account.
  - Baby profile: Baby's name, date of birth, sex, birth weight, birth height, and photo.
  - Activity logs: Feeding records (nursing duration, bottle volume, pump yield, solid foods), sleep sessions, diaper changes, growth
  measurements (weight, height, head circumference), health records (temperature, medications, doctor visits), and general activity
  logs.
  - Milestones: Developmental milestones your baby has achieved, including any photos or videos you attach.
  - Notes: Free-text notes you add to any log entry.

  Information Collected Automatically

  - Authentication tokens: Managed by Firebase Authentication to keep you signed in securely.
  - App usage data: Firebase may collect anonymized usage and performance data to help maintain service reliability. See
  https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy for details.

  Information from Third-Party Sign-In

  If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from those providers. We do not receive your password.
  Those sign-ins are governed by https://policies.google.com/privacy and https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ respectively.

  ---
  3. How We Use Your Information

  We use your information solely to provide and improve the BabySkillMap app:

  - Display your baby's profile, activity history, growth charts, and milestone tree.
  - Calculate XP, levels, streaks, and daily quests within the app.
  - Enable caregiver sharing so invited family members can view and log data for the same baby profile.
  - Send local push notifications (e.g., timer reminders) that you can enable or disable at any time in your device settings.
  - Respond to feedback or support requests you send to us.

  We do not use your information for advertising, sell it to third parties, or share it with any third party except as described in
  Section 4.

  ---
  4. How We Share Your Information

  Caregiver Sharing

  If you generate an invite code and share it with another person, that person gains access to your baby's profile data (including
  activity logs, milestones, and photos) for as long as the caregiver link is active. You can revoke an invite code at any time from
  the Profile screen. Caregivers you have already linked remain linked until they unlink themselves or you revoke their access.

  Service Providers

  We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  Provider: Google Firebase
  Purpose: Authentication, database (Firestore), and file storage
  Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  ────────────────────────────────────────
  Provider: Google (Sign in with Google)
  Purpose: Optional sign-in method
  Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

  No other third parties have access to your data.

  Legal Requirements

  We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request (e.g., a court order or
  government demand).

  ---
  5. Data Storage and Security

  - All data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Storage), hosted in the United States.
  - Data is encrypted in transit using TLS and encrypted at rest by Firebase.
  - Access to your data is controlled by Firebase Security Rules: only your account (and caregivers you have explicitly invited) can
  read or write your baby's data.
  - We do not store your data on any server we operate directly — Firebase is the sole data host.

  While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We
  encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

  ---
  6. Children's Privacy

  BabySkillMap is intended for use by parents and caregivers (adults aged 13 or older). The app collects information about babies at
  the direction of a parent or guardian, but is not directed at children themselves and does not knowingly collect personal
  information directly from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information about a child without appropriate
  parental consent, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

  ---
  7. Photos and Media

  Photos and videos you attach to milestones or activity logs are uploaded to Firebase Storage and associated with your account. They
  are accessible only to you and your linked caregivers. You can delete individual photos from within the app; deleted photos are
  permanently removed from Firebase Storage.

  ---
  8. Push Notifications

  BabySkillMap may send local push notifications (e.g., reminders to stop a running timer). These notifications are scheduled on your
  device and do not involve sending your data to a remote server. You can disable notifications at any time in your device's Settings
  app.

  ---
  9. Your Rights and Choices

  Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  - Access: View all data stored in your account from within the app.
  - Correction: Edit your account or baby profile at any time from the Profile screen.
  - Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data via Profile → Account Settings → Delete Account. Deletion is permanent and
  cannot be undone.
  - Data portability: Contact us to request an export of your data.
  - Withdraw consent: You can stop using the app and delete your account at any time.

  To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address in Section 11.

  ---
  10. Data Retention

  Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your data (profile, activity logs,
  photos, milestones) is permanently deleted from Firebase within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be linked back to
  you may be retained indefinitely for service improvement.
